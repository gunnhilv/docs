# Certificates

HUNT Cloud comply with two international managemenet systems, one for quality management (ISO 9001) and one for information security (ISO 27001).

Both certificates have been issued since 2017. For each certificate, independent auditors examines our governance, management, infrastructure, services and operations. Reports from these audits are available to data controllers on request.

# ISO 9001

ISO 9001:2015 Certificate (opens new window) (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in quality management «ISO 9001» is certified by Kiwa, Norway.

# ISO 27001

ISO 27001:2013 Certificate (opens new window) (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in information security management «ISO 27001 (opens new window)» is certified in accordance with our Statement of Applicability (SoA) by Kiwa, Norway.

ISO 27001:2013 Statement of Applicability (opens new window) (pdf)

Our Statement of Applicability do also include controls from «ISO 27017 (opens new window)» that covers security for cloud services, and «ISO 27018 (opens new window)» that covers protection of personal identifiable information in public clouds.
